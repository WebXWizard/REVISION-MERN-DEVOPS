# SRE Study Plan

SRE is the practice of applying software engineering to operations problems.

Focus areas:

- Reliability: keep user-facing services healthy.
- Observability: understand system behavior using signals.
- Automation: remove manual repetitive work.
- Incident response: restore service quickly and learn afterward.
- Capacity and performance: prepare systems for real traffic.
- Change safety: deploy without breaking users.

Suggested order:

1. SRE mindset and SLOs
2. Linux and networking troubleshooting
3. Observability and alerting
4. Incident management
5. Reliability engineering
6. Automation
7. Kubernetes and cloud operations
8. Release engineering
9. Capacity and performance
10. Security and compliance
11. Projects and mock interviews
