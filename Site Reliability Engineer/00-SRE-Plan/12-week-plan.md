# 12 Week SRE Plan

Week 1:

- SRE basics, SLOs, SLIs, SLAs, and error budgets.

Week 2:

- Linux troubleshooting, logs, systemd, CPU, memory, disk, and process debugging.

Week 3:

- Networking, DNS, TCP, TLS, HTTP, and load balancer debugging.

Week 4:

- Metrics, logs, traces, Prometheus, Grafana, and alerting.

Week 5:

- Incident response, severity levels, on-call, and postmortems.

Week 6:

- Reliability patterns, redundancy, retries, timeouts, circuit breakers, and disaster recovery.

Week 7:

- Bash and Python automation for SRE tasks.

Week 8:

- Kubernetes operations and debugging.

Week 9:

- CI/CD reliability, release strategies, rollback, and feature flags.

Week 10:

- Capacity planning, load testing, and performance analysis.

Week 11:

- Security, IAM, secrets, audit logs, and compliance basics.

Week 12:

- Build SRE projects, revise top questions, and practice mock interviews.
