# SLIs, SLOs, And SLAs

SLI means Service Level Indicator.

Examples:

- Request success rate
- p95 latency
- Availability percentage
- Queue processing delay
- Data freshness

SLO means Service Level Objective.

Example:

```txt
99.9 percent of API requests should complete successfully over 30 days.
```

SLA means Service Level Agreement.

An SLA is usually a business or legal promise made to customers.

Interview line:

```txt
SLI is the measurement, SLO is the target, and SLA is the external promise.
```
