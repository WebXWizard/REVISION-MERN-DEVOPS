# Disaster Recovery

Disaster recovery is the plan for restoring service after a major failure.

Important terms:

- RTO: Recovery Time Objective
- RPO: Recovery Point Objective

Example:

```txt
RTO 1 hour means service should be restored within 1 hour.
RPO 15 minutes means at most 15 minutes of data can be lost.
```

Practice:

- Test backups.
- Document restore steps.
- Verify failover.
- Run disaster recovery drills.
