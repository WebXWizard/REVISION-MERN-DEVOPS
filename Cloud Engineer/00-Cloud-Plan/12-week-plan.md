# 12 Week Cloud Engineer Plan

Week 1:

- Cloud basics, service models, deployment models, regions, availability zones, and shared responsibility.

Week 2:

- Linux, SSH, DNS, HTTP, TLS, firewall rules, and security basics.

Week 3:

- AWS IAM, users, groups, roles, policies, MFA, and least privilege.

Week 4:

- EC2, AMI, EBS, Auto Scaling, Elastic Load Balancing, and S3.

Week 5:

- VPC, subnets, route tables, internet gateway, NAT gateway, security groups, and NACL.

Week 6:

- RDS, DynamoDB, Lambda, API Gateway, SQS, SNS, and EventBridge.

Week 7:

- CloudWatch, CloudTrail, Config, backup, patching, and operations.

Week 8:

- Terraform, CloudFormation, Bash, Python, and CI/CD automation.

Week 9:

- Docker, ECR, ECS, Kubernetes, and EKS basics.

Week 10:

- High availability, disaster recovery, backup, restore, RTO, and RPO.

Week 11:

- Cost optimization, tagging, budgets, governance, compliance, and multi-cloud basics.

Week 12:

- Build projects, revise top questions, and practice mock interviews.
