# Developer Onboarding

Good onboarding should answer:

- How do I get access?
- How do I create a service?
- How do I run locally?
- How do I deploy?
- Where are logs and dashboards?
- Who owns this service?
- How do I ask for help?

Good onboarding output:

```txt
A developer can create, run, deploy, and monitor a simple service without asking many people.
```
