# Ingress And Service Mesh

Ingress:

- Routes external HTTP or HTTPS traffic to services.

Service mesh:

- Adds service-to-service traffic control and visibility.

Service mesh can provide:

- mTLS
- Traffic splitting
- Retries
- Timeouts
- Observability
- Policy enforcement

Tradeoff:

```txt
Service mesh adds power, but also operational complexity.
```
