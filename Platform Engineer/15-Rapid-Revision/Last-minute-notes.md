# Last Minute Notes

Remember:

- Platform Engineering is about developer experience and safe self-service.
- Developers are platform customers.
- Golden paths should be easy, documented, and supported.
- Guardrails should prevent risk without blocking normal work.
- Service catalogs need ownership metadata.
- Templates should include CI/CD, security, observability, and docs.
- GitOps keeps desired state in Git.
- IaC changes should be reviewed before apply.
- Platform SLOs should measure developer-facing capabilities.
- Adoption and feedback matter as much as technical elegance.
