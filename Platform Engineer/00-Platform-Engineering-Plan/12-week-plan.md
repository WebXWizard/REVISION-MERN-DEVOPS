# 12 Week Platform Engineer Plan

Week 1:

- Platform Engineering fundamentals, DevOps vs SRE vs Platform Engineering, platform as a product, and developer experience.

Week 2:

- Linux, networking, cloud basics, IAM, DNS, TLS, and production troubleshooting.

Week 3:

- Internal Developer Platform, golden paths, paved roads, developer onboarding, and documentation.

Week 4:

- Docker, Kubernetes architecture, deployments, services, ingress, storage, and cluster basics.

Week 5:

- Kubernetes platform operations, multi-tenancy, namespaces, RBAC, resource quotas, and platform add-ons.

Week 6:

- Terraform, modules, remote state, provisioning workflows, Crossplane basics, and automation patterns.

Week 7:

- CI/CD pipelines, reusable templates, release strategies, GitOps, Argo CD, and Flux basics.

Week 8:

- Observability, metrics, logs, traces, dashboards, alerts, runbooks, and platform SLOs.

Week 9:

- Secrets management, policy as code, image scanning, SBOM, supply chain security, and compliance.

Week 10:

- Service catalog, Backstage, scaffolding templates, ownership metadata, docs, and scorecards.

Week 11:

- Platform APIs, self-service workflows, reliability, scalability, cost controls, quotas, and capacity planning.

Week 12:

- Build projects, revise top questions, and practice mock interviews.
