# Platform Engineer Study Plan

Platform Engineering focuses on building reusable internal platforms that reduce developer friction.

Focus areas:

- Developer experience
- Internal Developer Platform
- Kubernetes platform operations
- Infrastructure as Code
- CI/CD and GitOps
- Security guardrails
- Observability
- Self-service workflows
- Service catalog and templates
- Platform product thinking

Suggested order:

1. Platform engineering fundamentals
2. Linux, networking, and cloud basics
3. Developer experience and IDP
4. Kubernetes and container platform
5. IaC and provisioning automation
6. CI/CD and GitOps
7. Observability and operations
8. Security, secrets, and compliance
9. Service catalog and templates
10. Platform APIs and self-service
11. Reliability, scalability, and cost
12. Team topology and platform product thinking
13. Projects and mock interviews
