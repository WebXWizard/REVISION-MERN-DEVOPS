# 12 Week Automation Architect Plan

Week 1:

- Automation fundamentals, strategy, architecture principles, ROI, risks, and governance.

Week 2:

- Process discovery, workflow mapping, candidate selection, requirements, and exception paths.

Week 3:

- Python, PowerShell, Bash, reusable scripts, configuration files, logging, and error handling.

Week 4:

- Workflow orchestration, scheduling, dependencies, approvals, retries, and human-in-the-loop design.

Week 5:

- API integration, webhooks, queues, event-driven design, idempotency, and integration patterns.

Week 6:

- Infrastructure automation with Terraform, Ansible, provisioning workflows, and drift control.

Week 7:

- CI/CD, release automation, deployment gates, rollback, and environment promotion.

Week 8:

- Cloud automation, serverless automation, Kubernetes automation, and platform self-service.

Week 9:

- RPA, low-code automation, bot governance, exception handling, and maintenance.

Week 10:

- Testing automation, regression suites, E2E tests, performance checks, and quality gates.

Week 11:

- Observability, operations automation, AIOps basics, runbooks, auto-remediation, and incident automation.

Week 12:

- Security, governance, audit, scalability, reliability, cost, projects, and mock interviews.
