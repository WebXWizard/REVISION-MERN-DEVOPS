# Rate Limiting

Rate limiting user/IP ke requests ko limit karta hai.

Why used:

- Abuse prevent karna
- Brute-force login prevent karna
- Server protection

Real-world example:

- Max 5 login attempts per minute.

Common mistakes:

- Auth endpoints par rate limit na lagana.

- What is rate limiting?
- Why use it on login APIs?
