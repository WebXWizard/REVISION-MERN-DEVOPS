# Projects Interview Questions

General questions:

1. Explain your project.
2. Why did you choose this project?
3. Why this architecture?
4. Why MongoDB?
5. Why React?
6. Why Express?
7. Biggest challenge?
8. What did you learn?
9. How would you scale it?
10. What would you improve?

Technical deep-dive:

1. Explain your folder structure.
2. Explain your database schema.
3. Explain your API flow.
4. Explain your auth flow.
5. How do you handle errors?
6. How do you protect routes?
7. How do you validate data?

Follow-up questions:

1. What if 10,000 users use your app?
2. What if DB query becomes slow?
3. What if token expires?
4. What if user uploads large files?
5. What if frontend and backend are on different domains?
