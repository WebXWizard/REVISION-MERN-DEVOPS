# Mock Interview Rules

Practice like a real company round.

Rules:

- One question at a time.
- Answer first, then check notes.
- Speak in 60 to 90 seconds.
- Give one real project example whenever possible.
- If you do not know, say what you know and how you would find the answer.

Scoring:

```txt
8-10: Clear, confident, example-based
6-7: Correct but missing depth
4-5: Partial understanding
0-3: Needs revision
```

Mini task:

- Record one answer and self-score it.
