# HR Round Resources

Prepare these:

- 60-second self introduction
- 3-minute project explanation
- Why MERN answer
- Why hire you answer
- Strength and weakness answer
- Short-term and long-term goals
- Relocation/shift/internship availability answer

Practice method:

- Speak out loud.
- Record yourself.
- Remove filler words.
- Keep answers honest and specific.
- Connect every answer to learning, projects, or role readiness.

Good fresher themes:

- Curious learner
- Strong fundamentals
- Project-based learning
- Team readiness
- Consistency
- Ownership
