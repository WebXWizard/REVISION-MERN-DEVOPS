# Weaknesses

Good weakness answer is honest but controlled.

Examples:

- I used to spend too much time perfecting UI, now I timebox tasks.
- I was less confident in DSA, now I practice daily.

Avoid:

- "I have no weakness."
- Critical weakness for role.

Answer format:

```txt
Earlier I struggled with ____. I noticed it during ____. Now I am improving it by ____.
```

Mini task:

- Write one honest weakness with improvement plan.
