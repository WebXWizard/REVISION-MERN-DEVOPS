# HR Round Study Notes

HR round checks communication, attitude, clarity, honesty, and company fit.

Important answers to prepare:

- Tell me about yourself
- Why MERN Stack?
- Why should we hire you?
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Future goals
- Explain your project
- Are you open to learning new tech?

Tell me about yourself structure:

```txt
Name + background
Skills
Projects
Current goal
Positive closing
```

Sample fresher answer:

```txt
Hi, my name is _____. I am a fresher MERN Stack developer. I have learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB. I have built projects where I worked on frontend components, REST APIs, MongoDB schemas, and authentication. Currently I am looking for an internship/fresher role where I can contribute, learn from senior developers, and improve as a full-stack developer.
```

Common mistakes:

- Memorized robotic answer.
- Too long introduction.
- Saying negative things about yourself.
- Not connecting skills with projects.
- Overclaiming experience.
