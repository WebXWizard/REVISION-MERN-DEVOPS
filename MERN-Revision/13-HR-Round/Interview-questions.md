# HR Round Interview Questions

Common questions:

1. Tell me about yourself.
2. Why should we hire you?
3. Why MERN Stack?
4. What are your strengths?
5. What are your weaknesses?
6. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
7. Explain your project.
8. What was your biggest challenge?
9. Are you comfortable working in a team?
10. Are you open to learning other technologies?

Behavioral questions:

1. Tell me about a time you solved a difficult bug.
2. Tell me about a time you learned something quickly.
3. How do you handle feedback?
4. What do you do when you are stuck?

Practice task:

- Write answers for top 5 questions.
- Keep each answer between 45 and 90 seconds.
