# Tell Me About Yourself

Structure:

```txt
Name/background -> Skills -> Projects -> Current goal -> Positive close
```

Sample:

```txt
Hi, I am _____. I am a fresher MERN Stack developer. I have learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB. I have built projects where I worked on UI, REST APIs, authentication, and database design. I am looking for an opportunity where I can contribute, learn from seniors, and grow as a full-stack developer.
```

Avoid:

- Too much family detail.
- Very long answer.

Mini task:

- Write your version in 120 words.
