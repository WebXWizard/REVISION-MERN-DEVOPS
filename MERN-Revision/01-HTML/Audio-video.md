# Audio and Video

HTML media tags browser me audio/video play karne ke liye use hote hain.

Tags:

- `audio`
- `video`
- `source`

Why used:

- Native media support without external player.

Real-world example:

- Course platform video lecture.
- Music preview audio player.

Common mistakes:

- `controls` attribute bhool jana.
- Fallback text na dena.
- Large video optimize na karna.

Interview questions:

- How do you add video in HTML?
- Why use multiple `source` tags?

Mini task:

- Add a video with controls, poster, and fallback text.
