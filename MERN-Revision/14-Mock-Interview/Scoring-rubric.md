# Scoring Rubric

Use this score after every answer.

```txt
10: Clear, correct, example, internal working, confident
8-9: Strong answer with minor gaps
6-7: Correct but lacks depth/example
4-5: Partial answer
0-3: Concept unclear
```

Check:

- Definition clear?
- Why used?
- Real example?
- Internal working?
- Mistake/tradeoff?

Mini task:

- Score 5 of your own answers honestly.
