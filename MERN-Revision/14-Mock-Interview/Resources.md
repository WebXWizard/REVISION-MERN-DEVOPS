How to practice:

- Use timer.
- Speak clearly.
- Do not rush.
- If stuck, explain what you know.
- Ask clarifying questions in coding rounds.

Self-review checklist:

- Was the definition clear?
- Did I give an example?
- Did I mention internal working?
- Did I avoid wrong claims?
- Was the answer under 90 seconds?
