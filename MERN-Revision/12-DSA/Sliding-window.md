# Sliding Window

Sliding window contiguous subarray/substr problems solve karta hai.

Why used:

- O(n^2) brute force ko O(n) me optimize karne ke liye.

Common examples:

- Maximum sum subarray of size k
- Longest substring without repeating characters

Common mistakes:

- Window shrink/expand logic confuse karna.

Mini task:

- Find maximum sum of subarray size 3.
